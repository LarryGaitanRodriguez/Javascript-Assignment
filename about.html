We like to say that we're Local and Connected

Founded in 2021 as a mock page for UNG's CSCI 3000 Web development course, Lake Lanier Web Development has provided ZERO local businesses in the north Georgia area with high-quality, modern websites. We've built simple HTML-CSS pages, portfolios, and copy-cats of other web pages. Therefore we are confident that we here at LLWEBDEV can provide your brand or local business with a high-quality, modern website too.

Where did we come from?

The founder and single employee of Lake Lanier Web Development is a university student at the University of North Georgia. UNG is located in Dahlonega, Georgia, around 50ish miles north of Atlanta, GA. The company was a result of a project. The company came about due to the owner being really meta with one of his assignments. eventually they became too invested into the idea and decided to take a risk and create the company. They established it September 15th, 2021.
